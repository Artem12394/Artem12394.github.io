Refuse

If the item is free and you don't need it just say no. Politely say no especially if it is plastic and do not buy things like plastic forks and plastic plates. Say no to plastic bags at a grocery store when the workers give you the groceries but if you forgot you reuse able bags then you can take the plastic bags and reuse then something else like trash bags or to carry other stuff around.

Reduce

We can reduce waste by bringing our own fabric bags when we go to the grocery store. That's how we can stop using plastic bags. We can also help reduce waste by not wasting paper and recycling more.

Reuse

You can reuse plastic bags by going with them to shop to take products and everything that you need. Also You can reuse water bottles to not throw it out just to refill it many times.We can reuse the papers that we are using for the school projects.

Recycle

Be sure that you know that wanted things are recyclable. Use a bag or box to store all your recyclables. Make sure your items are not contaminated with food or liquid. Recycle your items in place for it in your town.

Rot

Even though rotting can sometimes smell bad it is very important to rot in order to not make our earth dirty. Rotting does take lots of time but at the end you would be pretty satisfied with the end results and this type of soil is really good for planting fruits and vegetables. You can basically rot paper towels, food scraps, plant debris and solid paper .
